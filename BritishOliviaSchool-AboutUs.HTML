We have awesome faculty with a mission of PERFECTION

British Olivia School is located in Hoshiarpur, Punjab and is housed in numerous magnificent buildings. The imposing main building with a beautiful structure houses the offices and classes from class I to XII with well equipped class rooms, laboratories, play grounds etc. The splendid building with the towering edifice to its right is the fully air conditioned K.G. block which lodges all the classes of the kindergarten.

The Olive Hall (above) is the main building of the school.

Our VISION

To bring about an all round development in children and channelize their energies for excellence in every field. To inculcate in children good habits, social manners and above all discipline. To enlarge the mental horizon and broaden the outlook of the children so as to enable them to face the challenges of tomorrow. To develop in children the ability to express their thoughts and feelings in a confident manner, so as to bring out their hidden talents and develop their personalities. To develop in children trust and coincidence in their own capacity to learn so that they can become responsible citizens.
