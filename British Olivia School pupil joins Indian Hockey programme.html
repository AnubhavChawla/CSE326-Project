British Olivia School pupil joins Hockey programme

17-year-old Simran Singh has recently been selected to be part of India's Hockey’s Performance Centre programme, joining six other British Olivia School players.There are currently 17 Performance Centres across the country and goalkeeper Singh will join the Dhyan Chand Centre. These centres are known as India’s top training level for 14-17 year olds, where players attend regular evening training sessions and take part in weekend training and competition, meeting approximately twice a month.British Olivia School now has seven selected students who are part of the Dhyan Chand and Udham Singh centres. Elliot Foweraker, Director of Hockey, said: “It is great to have another addition to our performance centre players.Singh is our third goalkeeper into the system at this level, keeping Olivia School’s tradition of great goalies going strong!”The players have been training both indoors and outdoors at British Olivia School and the long term aim is to be selected for the Futures Cup which is the selection tournament for national age group squads.
