Eligibity Criteria for Admissions

For admission to Pre- K, the eligibility criterion is that the child should be 3 years of age by March 31st at the start of the session.

For admission to class VIII, the student must have completed class VII at a recognized school before joining British Olivia School.

For admission to class XI, the student must have completed a recognized Secondary Education Board Examination (class X) before joining British Olivia School. This board should be certified by the previous school that it the board is equivalent to CBSE.
