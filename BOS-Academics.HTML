BOS Difference

For the past years, thousands of parents have placed their trust in the British Olivia School. Today, nearly 1850 students from India and different countries attend British Olivia School

Trained to create vibrant learning environments, BOS teachers are at the very heart of the School. As one of the largest providers of private Pre School-12 education, BOS is able to recruit the very best education professionals from around the area and is among the largest employer of teachers in Hoshiarpur. BOS employs a team of more than 100 education professionals and staff who ensure that the highest standards of learning and teaching are met — and exceeded. Ongoing training and continuing professional development programmes ensure that BOS teachers remain at the forefront of educational thinking and practice.

Academic results at Olivia are consistently high, with BOS students achieving well above national averages for equivalent examinations. Students in BOS which are affiliated to CBSE Board consistently attain exam results that are significantly above the national average. Year on year, students from GIIS schools achieve top honors – nationally and regionally.
Uniquely, BOS has developed school models that meet the needs of any community, anywhere in the world. Through a variety of teaching methodology and curriculum, BOS provides families with a world of choice.
BOS creates learning environments that break traditional boundaries. A philosophy of continuous innovation influences the design of Olivia and guides ongoing improvements in our existing school.
BOS is committed to continuous development and innovation, driven by a constant dialogue, both formal and informal, with parents and educationists. Uniquely, BOS benefits from the sharing of best practice across the network, aimed primarily at enhancing the standards of learning and teaching.
The BOS quality assurance programme ensures that the highest possible standards of education are maintained in the school. Olivia use rigorous self evaluation programmes to ensure that teaching standards are high and students are provided with learning opportunities that are matched to their specific needs.
BOS believes that parents are an integral part of the school community. BOS’ parent relations programme supports the positive interaction between school and home, encouraging parents to participate in the education process
BOS has access to a range specialists and experts in the education sector. Their ongoing guidance in all areas related to school, provides invaluable support to BOS in-house teams who drive development of specific areas of school business.
